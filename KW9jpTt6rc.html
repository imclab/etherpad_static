Challenge 1
  • http://webcraft-rahim.posterous.com/71381240
  • Like the others said, maybe change it to something like "Create a blog and write three sentences about yourself." This will remove some of the mental friction involved with writing a blog post about one's self (which is typically difficult for most people), and will make it easier for people to read and comment other people's blogs. With only three sentences, it leaves the comments open for questions rather than comments, which will create conversations on the person's blogs. ("Wow, you went to New York, I'm going there next week. Where do you recommend I eat?" rather than "Cool story, bro."
  • I don't know about the others, but I felt some trepidation with creating a blog. I know companies like Tumblr and Posterous have made it easy, but in my mind I'm still thinking "ugh, this is going to be a pain." It'd be cool if there were one created for me already by P2PU that I could just "claim" and get going on. I know that's basically the same thing as Tumblr, but it seems easier in my mind.

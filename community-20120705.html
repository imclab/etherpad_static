      • Open  Webville: a suite of Thimble projects powered by School of Open/ CC -  under the theme of Open Web.  These are Thimble projects that also live in p2pu as a School of Open  Challenge, (which involves more peer to peer interaction and hopefully  some badges/assessments.)

You see, it’s  managed by Madame Lille a former french politician and robot who is  relaunching her career in a new direction so she can focus more on  velvet bird painting, organic producing and protecting the Open Web. In  this series of mini games Madame Lille has started putting together a  guide book for Open WebVille. Use your Open Web dexterity to help yer  fill it in.

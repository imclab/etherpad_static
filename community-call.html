CALL TOPICS EVERY MONTH

  • 1st and 3rd Thursday  - General Progress Reports:
  • Short calls, using the 4Ps and an I (Progress, Priorities, Problems, Process, Ideas) format that the Tech Call uses. Anyone is welcome, staff will be there for sure. Documentation process stays the same. 

  • 2nd Thursday of every month - Seminar
  • A longer discussion, in which research may be presented, guests talk to us, Schools give us updates, special projects are discussed. All are welcome, documentation will be shared, as well as any readings/videos/links distributed before and after the call. 
  • School of Open call held after Seminar call at 9am San Francisco time: http://pad.p2pu.org/p/school-of-open-call

  • Last Thursday of every month - All Hands. 
  • We work, for an hour, together, on something. It might be tagging or de-spamming, writing static copy or dong course reviews. If you have something to suggest for one of these sessions, let us know on the Etherpad.

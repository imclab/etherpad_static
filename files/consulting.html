Option 1
Member-owend cooperative consulting group with P2PU brand
  • Form a member-owned LLC called something explicitly related to P2PU (e.g. P2PU Consulting Corps)
  • Members of the cooperative keep the majority of the fees they collect from any given project for themselves and invest 10-20% of those fees back into the cooperative automatically.  
  • That contribution will be used to cover fixed business expenses like licensing, registration, domain fees, etc.  Other larger investments (media production, marketing projects, etc.) will go through a member vote of the cooperative.
  • Additionally LLC commits X% of profit to P2PU through its charter

About the Job

Community is the heart of P2PU. We are seeking a sociable, community-minded individual to serve our community of life long learners. The right person for our community has a knack for supporting people and projects, lives on the web, and is passionate about innovating the future of education. 

See "Apply" below for application details.

About P2PU - Why we're here and how we're changing the world:

  • P2PU  is an online community for learning anything with your peers. We're recognized as a 501(c)3 non-profit public charity in the United States.
  • Over 20,000 individuals use P2PU. Our community is global, multi-lingual, and incredibly passionate about learning.
  • We're  funded by respected foundations and [top industry partners. We have a great staff supporting an  [amazing community of volunteers.
  • P2PU values openness, community, and peer learning. We're changing the way people learn from each other online.

Responsibilities - How you'll spend your time:

  1. Happy community
    1. Support our community of learners by facilitating their "first 30 days" at P2PU, directing them to resources, developing help content, and fostering community discussions. Attentiveness and problem solving are key.
    2. Represent the "typical user" in design and UX discussions to shape the development of the platform.
    3. Manage the weekly community call.
    4. Take the temperature of the community, chime in when needed to maintain good spirit, motivation, and enthusiasm.
    5. Occasional event planning for community workshops / meet-ups.
  2. Better courses with active users
    1. Work with the Learning Lead in guiding community members through the course design and assessment process. Connect course designers to resources and communitites of interest. 
    2. Act as overall curator of courses on P2PU. Promote good courses, help bad ones get better, get rid of spam.
    3. Promote P2PU courses individually and through larger promotions like "rounds of courses"
  3. Grow the P2PU community
    1. Use social media to get the word out and draw people in. Represent P2PU at meetings and conferences.
    2. Actively showcase the work of the P2PU community to the world by developing and maintaining our presence on the web through our blog, email newsletter, social networks.
    3. Act as first point of contact for communication coming into P2PU.
  4. Occasional project management and writing for special projects, including grant applications.

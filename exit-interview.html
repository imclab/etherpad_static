Questions to ask staff who are leaving:

  • What would be the one piece of advice that you would give to someone starting in your job? (What's the advice you would have liked to have when you started?)

  • What was the best thing about working for P2PU?

  • What was the most difficult thing about working for P2PU?

  • Did you have everything you needed to be successful?

  • Do you feel good about your accomplishments? Did you get satisfaction from your work?

  • Was your work challenging? Did you have opportunities to grow?

  • If you ever felt frustrated or unhappy, were there opportunities for you to express your thoughts? Were they taken seriously?

  • If you could have changed one thing about the work at P2PU, what would it be?

  • Are certain kind of people more likely to enjoy working at P2PU? What are their characteristics?

  • If you could have redesigned your job at P2PU to be exactly what you wanted it to be, what would you have changed?

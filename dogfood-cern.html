  • Private Courses
    • Courses that require privacy/confidentiality; for example a course on couselling people who have experienced trauma which invites participants to share their emotions after a traumatic experience. p2pu needs to avoid the liability that could arise through inadvertant disclosure of confidential material. On the other hand if everything is visible to everyone then some communities can't use p2pu. The best solution seems to be to instruct course organisers that if the course is going to ask participants to share sensitive information that participants should be warned at sign up to generate an email that protects their identities, and then to use user names that protect their identities. p2pu will then not have any private information, nor be able to link a person's actual identity to their participant identity.

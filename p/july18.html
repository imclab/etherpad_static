Build the Last Language Textbook
https://p2pu.org/en/groups/build-the-last-language-textbook/
Create 100% free and re-mixable language lessons to teach the world your language.
We are learning how to build media-rich interactive language lessons  online and working together to create a short curriculum of these  lessons for teaching basic English. All materials and software used are  100% free. Be part of the campaign to jumpstart collaborative languge  education online (thelastlanguagetextbook.org) and learn how to share your language with the world.

Curating Content
https://p2pu.org/en/groups/curating-content/
Get started organizing online content and explore curation tools in Curating Content. 
How many times have you tried to check a quick fact online and then lost  an hour following links and checking unhelpful websites? The  proliferation of paid search results and content mills makes it  difficult to find what you want. Like traditional museum and gallery  curators, digital curators acquire useful works and artifacts that fit  into their collections. Influential tech evangelist Robert Scoble has  said that content curation will be an essential 21st century digital  skill.
  • I love this course. 

MIT App Inventor & Explorer

Exploring the MIT App Inventor service to create Android Apps

3D Printing Ninja
https://p2pu.org/en/groups/3d-printing-ninja-part-1-internship/
Take baby steps to learn how 3D Printing works and take an idea  from zero to reality, fabricate & manufacture at home! Understand  how the flow works.      
Technology is here to stay and will forever change our life.  Manufacturing and DIY at home with the aid of technology is a reality  TODAY. Even 9 year old kids have been using 3D Printers to make robots,  sculptures, and beyond. Do not fear, zero computer science and 3D background required. You will earn the 3D Printing badge (in dev) after acquiring the basic  skills necessary to create a 3D model and get it printed and become  reality. With the help of an online browser based 3D modeling tool, we  will be able to create 3D jewels and sculpture in no time. Learn the skills before it become mandatory. Be a stealth ninja ahead of the chase.

Use Data to Show How Your Country is Unique! 
https://p2pu.org/en/groups/1-use-data-to-show-how-your-country-is-unique/
Take your first steps as a data wrangler. Search, find, retrieve,  analyze and present data that shows something unique about your country.     
Data wrangling is a universe of interlocking disciplines, skills and  bizarre terminology into which you fit somwhere. It may seem daunting to  get started, but this course will help you realize how much you know  already. You are able to do a bit of 'wrangling' already. By runninng  through a few straightforward tasks, you'll download data, find  interesting things in the data, and share them with your peers.

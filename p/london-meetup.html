Issue 1: For dyslexics, the content of the website is in your face. 

There is a danger in answering: everybody dislikes the way content is presented. Andrew will come back and think about how information can be better presented. 

Issue2: synchronicity iussues. A: I learn and perform best when there is interaction in real time. And it is difficult when people are in different time zones. Some courses suffer from a lack thereof.  http://p2pu.lighthouseapp.com/projects/71002-lernanta/tickets/4-timezones-support

There are degrees of interaction that courses take though. A: I find myself often overwhelmed by forum and mailling list communication. 
Those spaces are indeed not really how people interact. But you can point out to course organisers that you can design a course according to the needs of the students that are drawn to yuor course, not necessarily according to the way you would like to teach that course. 

Many to many communication is difficult.

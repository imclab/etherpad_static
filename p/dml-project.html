From email: This grant will result in P2PU producing an open source, customizable, fully documented platform that will support and guide people in creating Mozilla OBI-compliant badge systems. This platform will make it easy for badges issuers to develop badges while incorporating peer learning and innovative assessment into their systems. 


P2PU will build a generic learning assessment and badge issuing application that can be used for courses and badges on virtually any topic. The issuer functionality is available to anyone interested in issuing badges:
- P2PU will host a version for use by anyone free of charge on the p2pu.org learning platform. 
- The issuer is developed as a django app (module) that can easily be integrated into other django based applications.
- We will provide well documented code and instructions to support integration into other applications.

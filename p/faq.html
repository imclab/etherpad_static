Q: Do all course materials need to be freely and openly available?
A: All materials referenced in a study group or course need to be available to anyone legally at no charge. Even if materials you link to are not openly licensed, as long as anyone can get them free of charge that is usually ok. In some rare instances, study groups have to make use of materials that are not freely available (e.g. a literature course that includes a book that is currently under copyright) but those are the exception.

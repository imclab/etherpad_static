  1. Introductions + Overview - 30 min
    • Class of 2012
      • What aspect of openness do you want to teach or learn with others in your region?
      • For newbies: Name a time when openness helped you.
    • Overview, Q&A 
  2. Discuss each region's education needs - 30 min
    • What is your region's education needs around "open"? eg. licensing, policy, OER?
    • Who are you trying to help? (transition from large group to break-out)
      • See http://pad.p2pu.org/p/school-of-open-guidelines
  3. Find and/or outline resources to help that person
    • Option 1: In groups of 2-4 take this challenge together: https://p2pu.org/en/groups/teach-someone-something-with-open-content/
    • Option 2: Wing it - look through http://pad.p2pu.org/p/school-of-open-resources and start adapting existing resources
  4. Write ideal course description for that person
    • Catchy title
    • 1-2 sentence description
  5. Closing circle
    • Register for a P2PU account and start creating: https://p2pu.org/es/groups/create/
      • You can also create in beta: http://beta.p2pu.org/es/
    • Join the discussion list and introduce yourself: https://groups.google.com/group/school-of-open. Send a note when you're ready for feedback!

It's actually rather easy to know if you are giving feedback on the new course UX or something else. If the URL starts with http://alpha.p2pu.org/en/courses/ then you are in the right place!

So, after all these words, lets get started. Log in using "user-1" with "password". Head over to http://alpha.p2pu.org/en/courses/create and create a new course. The help text in the column on the right hand side needs to be updated, so suggestions would be welcome.

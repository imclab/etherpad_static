FRAMING QUESTIONS
Here are some guiding questions to help think about presenting your topic or project you’re involved in to help shape our discussion at the September meeting.
(p2pu and WebMadeMovies pdf’s attached as example roadmaps)
 
1.       What is your universal subtitle? I.e. if your project was a movie, what would you name it?
2.       What’s the problem that we’re trying to solve?
3.       How are you going to solve it?
4.       What’s worth underscoring; the unique value? 
5.       How is the finished product going to improve the world?
6.       What are the 3-5 ways the reader/listener can participate in helping the project succeed?
